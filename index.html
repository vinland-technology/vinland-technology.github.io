Hello! This blog is to introduce Vinland Technology. We're a small company who build free software tools to help in the process of Free and Open Source (FOSS) license compliance. Our goal is to be developer centric, which means we focus compliance efforts where we think it has the most impact; on the developer level. After all, it is software developers who're most comfortable with source code, and giving them the process, tooling, and understanding of licensing enables them to build communities around their code as well as provide for commercial opportunities.
